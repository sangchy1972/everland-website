Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Overview

At Everland Technology Limited, we want you to be satisfied with our applications and services. This Refund & Return Policy explains when and how you may be eligible for a refund.

2. Mobile Application Purchases

2.1 iOS App Store Purchases

For applications purchased through the Apple App Store:

  • Refund requests must be submitted directly to Apple
  • Apple's refund policy applies to all App Store purchases
  • Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund
  • Refund eligibility is determined by Apple at their discretion

2.2 Google Play Store Purchases

For applications purchased through Google Play Store:

  • You may request a refund within 48 hours of purchase
  • Refund requests must be submitted through Google Play
  • Visit the Google Play Help Center for refund instructions
  • After 48 hours, refund requests are handled at Google's discretion

3. In-App Purchases

For in-app purchases and subscriptions:

  • Refund policies are governed by the respective app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store)
  • Consumed in-app purchases (e.g., virtual goods that have been used) may not be eligible for refund
  • Subscription cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period

4. Consulting Services

For information consulting and custom development services:

  • Before Service Begins: Full refund available if cancelled more than 7 days before scheduled service date
  • After Service Begins: Refunds prorated based on work completed
  • Completed Services: No refunds available for completed consulting engagements
  • Custom Development: Refunds evaluated on a case-by-case basis according to project milestones

5. Subscription Services

For recurring subscription services:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No refunds for partial billing periods
  • You will continue to have access until the end of the paid period

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally non-refundable:

  • Downloaded software that has been activated or used
  • Consumed in-app purchases or virtual goods
  • Services that have been fully performed
  • Custom development work that has been delivered and accepted
  • Purchases made more than 30 days ago (subject to app store policies)

7. How to Request a Refund

7.1 App Store Purchases

For purchases made through app stores, please contact the respective store directly:

  • Apple App Store: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google Play Store: Through your Google Play account settings

7.2 Direct Purchases and Services

For consulting services or direct purchases, contact us at:

  • Email: admin@everlandapps.com
  • Response Time: We will respond within 5 business days
  • Required Information:
    • Your name and contact information
    • Order/transaction number
    • Date of purchase
    • Reason for refund request
    • Any supporting documentation

8. Refund Processing Time

Once your refund is approved:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • App Store Purchases: According to store policy (typically 3-5 business days)
  • Other Payment Methods: 7-14 business days

9. Exchanges

We do not offer exchanges for downloaded applications. If you experience technical issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

10. Price Changes

Prices for our applications and services may change at any time. Price changes do not affect existing subscriptions until the next billing cycle.

11. Promotional Offers

Refunds for promotional or discounted purchases will be calculated based on the actual amount paid, not the regular price.

12. Disputes

If you have a dispute regarding a refund:

  1. Contact our support team first to attempt resolution
  2. If unresolved, you may contact your payment provider
  3. For app store purchases, follow the store's dispute resolution process

13. Policy Changes

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Information

For questions about this Refund Policy or to request a refund, please contact us:
